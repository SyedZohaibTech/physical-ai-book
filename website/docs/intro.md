# Welcome to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

This textbook is an introduction to the exciting world of building intelligent robots that can perceive and interact with the physical world.

This book will cover:
- The fundamentals of ROS 2
- Simulating robots in Gazebo and Unity
- Advanced perception and navigation with NVIDIA Isaac
- Connecting large language models to robots (VLA)

Let's get started!
